MGHS Boys Basketball Tryouts November 4, 2020


Good afternoon Miller Grove Student-Athletes and Parents, It’s that time of year again! Boys’ basketball tryouts will be Wednesday, November 4, 2020 from 5:15pm – 7:15pm. Students are to report at 5:00pm for check-in each day. Tryouts will be held that Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday in the gym for both varsity and junior varsity athletes. Athletes must be dressed for tryouts, NO locker room usage/ access. Everyone must have a mask to enter and bring their own water bottle and temperature will be taken at the door. No spectators are allowed in the gym, only athletes that’s trying out will be allowed.
All students MUST have these 5 REQUIRED forms completed and filled out before they can enter the gym for tryouts.
Athletic Physical
Return Participation Form
Cardiac Arrest Form
Concussion Form
Emergency Medical Card
